## DB9 (EIA-574)

MALE (exposed pins) Pin numbering:

-   5 pin on top, 1-5 LEFT to RIGHT
-   4 pin on bottom, 6-9 LEFT to RIGHT

FEMALE (accepts pins) Pin numbering:

-   5 pin on top, 1-5 RIGHT to LEFT
-   4 pin on bottom, 6-9 RIGHT to LEFT

D-sub 9 pin (DB9/DE9)

| Pin | Purpose                      | Color |
